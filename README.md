# ‚ùÑÔ∏è Snowtype

**Bring the magic of a winter snowfall to your macOS desktop.** Snowtype is a lightweight, elegant, and completely free application that renders a serene animation of gently falling snowflakes directly over your desktop wallpaper and windows.

## ‚ú® Features

*   **Realistic Snowfall:** Enjoy a beautifully simulated snow animation with natural movement and accumulation effects.
*   **Ultra-Lightweight:** Built with performance in mind. It uses minimal system resources so your Mac runs smoothly.
*   **Desktop-Integrated:** Snowflakes appear *behind* your application windows but *over* your wallpaper, creating a perfect layered effect.
*   **Simple & Intuitive:** Launch the app, and it just works. A menu bar icon gives you quick control to show or hide the snowfall.
*   **Completely Free & Native:** A labor of love, built 100% in Swift for the macOS platform. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs.

## üöÄ Why Snowtype?

I created Snowtype because I wanted a simple, beautiful winter aesthetic for my desktop but couldn't find a **free, native, and non-intrusive** alternative. Many options were outdated, resource-heavy, or locked behind paywalls.

This app is my answer: a modern, efficient, and delightful utility written entirely in Swift, harnessing the full power of the macOS frameworks for a seamless experience.

## üì¶ Installation

1.  Download the latest release from the [Releases](../../releases) page.
2.  Drag `Snowtype.app` to your `Applications` folder.
3.  Launch the app. Grant necessary permissions if prompted (for desktop drawing).
4.  The snow begins to fall! Control it via the **‚ùÑÔ∏è** icon in your menu bar.
